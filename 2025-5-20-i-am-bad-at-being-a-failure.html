I Am Bad at Being a Failure

Since my childhood i have always feared to do something. this fear was about what people would say or think about me but also about what if i'm not ready what if i am not that good what if i fail.

wasted time to think and reminisce the success i could achieve and feared the potential of losing, and here i am BEHOLD a FAILURE , what i hated the most.

Sabotage

This line of thinking will put you at a emotional state that is so fragile a fart would break you.

After your tenth rejection you will be a push over that thinks has no place in world. this will perpetuate through your life. you try to save face and be on good behavior like a good dog that listens to every command.

It will show up everywhere and makes you miserable. You will save face and save yourself from cringe but it will stops you from being comfortable in you own skin.

Novelty

Your knees will be shaking at the thought of new experiences , entering a running competition would give you a heart attack (not really im trying to be funny and failing (that was so meta)).

If you don't take a chance you have already failed .

And what about learning new things , that means failing like you are a school kid.

An Unjust Judge

You will feel every word and every sentence coming out of your mouth, you will think every action you take has a inspector .

Well you are correct , you are the inspector and will always look at yourself with harsh eyes.

It is good to have a critical view of your skills and capabilities but in no way you should be attached to your view.

And best way to examine yourself is by testing, and testing means failing. so if you never test yourself you will never know who you are.

Encouragement

When you keep failing and failing constantly you will believe that you are a failure. You had expectations for how things would go and those expectations were not egregious . But reality proven even worse than what you imagined.

If you had problems with self esteem and fear of failure like me bouncing back from this stage will be a nightmare. And let me tell when you are falling NO ONE will catch you they just bring up how stupid the whole idea was.

You will not get any encouragements .

This is why you should care lees about expectations of yourself and others otherwise moving on and growing will be impossible.

"Do you know who a real loser is ? A real loser is somebody that is so afraid of not winning they don't even try. " -Little Miss Sunshine

i am miserable and scared and lost. and the worst part i know it and still keep at it. don't be like me.
